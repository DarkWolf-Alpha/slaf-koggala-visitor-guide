Welcome to Visitors of Sri Lanka Air Force Koggala Station Holiday Homes

Nestled 14 km south of Galle, SLAF Koggala Station offers stunning views of the Koggala Lagoon and the sea, with comfortable holiday homes for personnel and guests. This guide helps you explore nearby attractions in Southern Sri Lanka while enjoying your stay at this historic site, rich in WWII heritage and modern recreational facilities.
